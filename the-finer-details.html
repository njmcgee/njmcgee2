I'm Nathan McGinness

I design and develop websites, logos, print work, animations and motion graphics.

Employment

Designer, Icelab, ACT 2006-2009

This small boutique studio provided me with three years of experience in high quality design across many mediums. I developed top class front-end development skills and further developed the higher level programming skills I developed at university. The studio's size allowed me to work on a wide range of projects in all aspects – pitching, design, development, project management and deployment.

Lecturer and Tutor, University of Canberra, ACT 2006-2008

I provided lectures in Design and Media Arts, and co-designed two new courses: Networked Media Production and Interface Design and Visualisation.

Research Assistant, University of Canberra, ACT 2006

I helped deliver a research project on web 2.0 technologies in higher education called Digital Learning Communities (DLC): investigating the application of social software to support networked learning. This was published in 2008.

VJ / Production Assistant, Academy Club, ACT 2006

Provided live visuals, lighting, and maintenance of multimedia hardware.

Education

University of Canberra, ACT - Honours in Creative Communication

12 months worth of creative research into the audio-visual relationship with Dr. Mitchell Whitelaw. I produced a seven minute generative animation and an exegesis entitled The Audiovisual Illusion.

Bachelor of Communications (Media / Multimedia Production)

Majored in Creative Writing and New Media

Other interests and endeavours

Art

My generative artworks featured in the Dorkbot CBR exhibition at Canberra Contemporary Artspace and at the John Paynter Gallery in Newcastle as part of Electrofringe. My favourite artists include Josef Albers and Sol LeWitt.

Film & music

I directed, filmed and edited a documentary about US band Elliott's last days. It was released with Photorecording on Revelation Records. In 2005 I made a record called Hell or High Water. I also love radio as a medium, and worked in commercial radio and presented a show on 2XX Community radio.

Sport

I'm a passionate South Sydney Rabbitohs supporter, a keen fisherman and enjoy playing cricket on weekends.
